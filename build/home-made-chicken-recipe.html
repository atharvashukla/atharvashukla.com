Home-made Chicken Recipe

  • dalchini
  • javitri
  • “star”masala
  • patthar phool
  • jayaphal
  • kali mirchi, long
  • jira
  • tomatoes
  • tej patta
  • kaju, half a bowl
  • opnion, 4-5
  • lehsun, half bowl
  • adrak, 1-2 pieces
  • chicken, full, local - - - - - - - - -
  • cut up the onion
  • add oil to pan
  • add jira + patti + onion to pan
  • grind adrak and add
  • grind lehsun and add
  • (add adrak, lehsun paste after the onion is brown)
  • then add kaju
  • add tomatoes
  • add salt, 1-2 spoons (big spoon not table spoon)
  • add dhaniya, 2 tbsp
  • add mirchi, 1.5 tbsp
  • add haldi, 1-2 tbsp
  • add water, 1 glass, keep mixing
  • about 10-15 mins
  • let it cool (in water)
  • then add to mixer
  • add some oil to kadhai
  • add kashmiri laal, with some water mixed in
  • add tej patta
  • add masala and cover it. this will take some time. - - - - - - - - -
